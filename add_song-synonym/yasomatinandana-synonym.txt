amala harinām = these pure holy names; amiya vilāsā = are full of sweet, nectarean pastimes; vipina-purandara = He is the monarch of the garden groves; navīna nāgara-bora = He is the best of youthful lovers; vaḿśīvadana = He is seen with His flute placed to His lips; suvāsā = He is an excellent dresser.
